Project Overview
- **Product:** Modern bespoke B2B website for a digital design and marketing agency.
- **Goal:** To attract future clients and showcase advanced front-end engineering, fluid animations, and robust form handling.
- **Audience:** Tech founders, marketing directors, and FlyRank recruiters & mentors.

Tech Stack & Constraints
- **Backend**: Javascript, 
- **Frontend**: React, Tailwind CSS, HTML & CSS, Vite 7.2
- **AI Integration**: Vercel AI SDK
- **Framework:** Next JS 14/15
- **Animations:** Framer Motion (Keep animations smooth, performant, and under 0.4s), GSAP.
- **Forms:** React Hook Form + Zod (Strict schema validation for all client discovery forms).
